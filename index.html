About

I started in mechanical engineering, working on vehicle modeling and decarbonization at Argonne National Laboratory. Over time, I found myself gravitating toward analyzing data, building pipelines, and workflows that supported simulation and validation work.
I worked on projects like AutonomieAI, helping integrate machine learning into large-scale simulation workflows, and developed production-ready data pipelines in Python and MATLAB.
I completed a Master’s in Computer Science focusing on machine learning and applied statistics. I am passionate about using AI to find innovative, practical solutions to real-world problems.
