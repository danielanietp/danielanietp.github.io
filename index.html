About

I spent seven years at Argonne National Laboratory modeling vehicles, researching decarbonization, and building the data pipelines and workflows that made that work possible.
As that work grew more analytical and development-heavy, I went back to school for a Master's in Computer Science to deepen my foundation in machine learning and applied statistics.
Now I'm looking to bring that into a role where the work is purposeful, the problems are hard, and the solutions reach people at scale.
