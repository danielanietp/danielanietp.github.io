About

  • Resume

I'm a data analyst with 7+ years of experience turning complex, messy datasets into clear decisions. At Argonne National Laboratory, I built end-to-end data pipelines, maintained large-scale databases, and developed Tableau dashboards that shaped strategy for DOE and industry stakeholders.

My work has spanned the full data lifecycle — from querying and cleaning raw simulation outputs to engineering features, automating workflows, and communicating findings through reports and peer-reviewed research.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from UIUC, with coursework in database systems, statistical learning, and data cleaning. I bring both the technical depth to work with large, high-dimensional datasets and the communication skills to make findings accessible to any audience.

Now I'm looking to bring that into a role where the work is purposeful, the problems are hard, and the solutions reach people at scale.
